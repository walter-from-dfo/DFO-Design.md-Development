How we use primitives

A single shared pipeline takes raw evidence (L0) through locked rules (L1–L2), gets filled at runtime (L2.5), is rendered (L3), and is reviewed by the judges (L4). Read top-down to follow the flow.

L0
Evidence
Raw source materials from the product team. Never edited, never interpreted — primary sources only.
What lives here Logo · Color_Palette · Shapes · Sparkles · Brand fonts · Product photos · Before/after photos · Doctor portrait
curated into
L1
Primitives
Immutable brand truth — facts, not interpretations. Locked once, inherited by every project automatically.
What's locked global-rules.md · color-system.md · reusable-elements.md — typography, two-tone header, sparkles, wave shapes, accent hex per SKU.
consumed by
L2
Locked judgment
11 template .md files — one decision per carousel slot. Locks layout variant, element rules, and what "correct" looks like.

filled at runtime

Production
Four modalities can render. The filled prompt + reference image are required inputs in every case — never one without the other.

Finished carousel images. Every candidate is reviewed by the judges before it's saved — fail traces back to the layer that caused it.
